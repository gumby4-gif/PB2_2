Designing with Native Plants

Published: March 2026  ·  Category: Planting Plans

Pros of Using Native Plants

Native plants offer valuable support to local wildlife by providing natural food sources and habitat. They help protect soil from erosion and make efficient use of water. Because they typically require less irrigation and fewer fertilizers, they can lower maintenance costs while offering meaningful environmental benefits. Native plants are also less susceptible to pests, contributing to a healthier, more resilient landscape. Overall, they promote biodiversity and help maintain a balanced, thriving ecosystem.
